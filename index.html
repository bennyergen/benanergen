About Myself
Benan Ergen

Hi. I'm Benan Ergen, a freelance UI & UX designer based in Turkey.
I am a passionate minimalistic driven creative designer seeking to bring meaning & simplicity to Design & Technology. My passions for the UI/UX Design industries can be linked back to my childhood, where I would constantly research new emerging technologies, compare current technologies and look at how it would affect myself.
I really like getting into the nitty-gritty technical details of all IT/Design aspects.
